### Need to work on:

#### 1. The J Sound (Jota)
Correction: In Jardín, you wrote zhar-DEEN.
The Fix: In Spanish, J is never a "soft J/Z" sound (like azure or Jacques). It is always a breathy, raspy H (like a strong English Hot).
New Guide: Jardín — har-DEEN.

#### 2. Syllable Breaks & Diphthongs
Correction: In Esquiar, you wrote ehs-kwee-ahr.
The Fix: Since QU is a digraph with a silent U, the "W" sound disappears completely. New Guide: Esquiar — ehs-kyahr or es-kee-ar.

#### 3. The Double C (CC)
Correction: In Reacción, you wrote ree-ahk-SYON.
The Fix: While the sound is correct, the first vowel is an E (reh), not an I (ree). Spanish vowels are very "pure" and don't change.
New Guide: Reacción — reh-ahk-SYON.

#### 4. Vowel Consistency
Correction: In Esfinge, you wrote ehs-fihn-heh.
The Fix: Spanish I always sounds like the "ee" in bee.
New Guide: Esfinge — es-FEEN-heh.
