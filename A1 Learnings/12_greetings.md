# Greetings

### Hola
    Hello
        - Used any time of the day
        - Any situation, formal or informal
    
### Buenos dias
    Good morning
        - Time you wake up/Sunrise until 12pm

### Buenas tardes
    Good afternoon
        - 12pm (or after lunch) to 8pm
    
### Buenas noches
    Good evening/night
        - Sunset (around 8pm) to sunrise

### Adios
    Goodbye

### Si
    Yes

### No
    No
