# Manners

### Por favor
    please
        - Literally "for favor" or "as a favour"
        
### Gracias
    thank you
        grah-thyas
        grah-syas

### ¿Cómo estás?
    How are you?
        KOH-moh es-TAHS?
        - Without the opening ¿, a reader wouldn't know to raise their pitch
                until they hit the very end of the sentence.

### Bien / Estoy bien.
    Good / I'm good.
        - Well/Alright / I'm doing well.

### Bastante bien / Estoy bastante bien.
    Pretty good / I'm doing pretty well.

### No, gracias.
    No, thanks.

### Así así / Tirando               / Regular
    So-so    / Hanging on/Getting by / Normal/Average/Nothing special
        - así así = most neutral
        - tirando = Literally means "to pull".
                "Pulling through".
                Surviving.
        - regular = Slightly negative.
                Underwhelming.
    
### Mal
    Bad

### ¿Y tú?
    And you?
