# Possessive word RULES

### Mi / Mis
    My
        - must match number of objects

        Mi  caballo
        Mis caballos

### Tu / Tus
    Your
        - Tu (your) vs Tú (you)

        Tu  amigo
        Tus amigos

### Su
    Their / His / Her
        - one word covers all three
        - (reminds me of Tagalog "niya")

        Su  casa
        Sus casas

### Ellos / Ellas
    They
        - Use Ellos for any group with a male
